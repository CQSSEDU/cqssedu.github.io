Who we are?

We are the Student Council of the Canada Qingdao Secondary School,

We represent and serve all students.

Specifically, the Student Council will be responsible for planning student activities, providing life and academic support to students, and deciding student affairs.

The 2023-2024 Student Council of Canada Qingdao Secondary School has officially established after a series of democratic election procedures. You can see File #PMAN2301 for more detils about Student Council members and their positions.

What we do?

The Student Council will continually aim to improve the student learning environment, plan more educational student activities and serve the overall interests of the students. We will collaborate with schools and teachers to promote student development as always.

Components of the Student Council

The Student Council has an Executive Team, which contains the Prime Minister of the Student Council, and two Deputy Prime Ministers.

There are several Student Council departments as follows:

Office of Secretary:

Responsible for taking minutes of student council meetings and issuing student council announcements.

Treasury:

Manages the funds of the Student Council.

Ministry of Academics:

Responsible for academic matters concerning students.

Ministry of Art:

Responsible for organising art festivals and promoting students' artistic qualities.

Ministry of Sports:

Responsible for organising sports competitions and enhancing students' physical fitness.

Ministry of Media:

Responsible for the school's multimedia affairs, including photography, video editing, and multimedia management during events.

Ministry of Health:

Responsible for monitoring epidemics, reminding students to take precautionary measures in time, and promoting health knowledge on a regular basis.
