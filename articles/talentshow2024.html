CQSS Shines in Summer Talent Show 2024

CQSS在2024年夏季才艺秀中大放异彩

Reported by Levi Ji, Special Correspondent

The CQSS 2024 Summer Talent Show drew a large crowd with a series of spectacular performances showcasing the various talents of the school's students. The event, hosted by the Student Council, highlighted the school's commitment to fostering creativity and community spirit.

In an exclusive interview, we caught up with Eric Zhang, Student Council Prime Minister, and Sophie Yang, Talent Show Director, to get an insight into the hard work that goes on behind the scenes of the event.

CQSS 2024年夏季才艺秀吸引了大量学生群体，现场表演精彩纷呈，展示了学生们的各种才华。此次活动由学生会主办，突显了学校对培养创造力和自我领导力的重视。

在本次独家采访中，我们采访了学生会主席 Eric Zhang 和才艺秀导演 Sophie Yang，深入了解了活动幕后工作的点滴。

A Night to Remember 难忘之夜

The auditorium at CQSS was packed to the rafters as students and teachers gathered for the much-anticipated Summer 2024 Talent Show. The evening featured an exciting programme of musical performances, dances, and musical skits, each of which reflected the unique skills and enthusiasm of the students.

CQSS的礼堂座无虚席，学生和老师们齐聚一堂，期待已久的2024年夏季才艺秀如期举行。晚上节目精彩纷呈，有音乐表演、舞蹈和音乐短剧，每一个节目都反映了学生们独特的技能和热情。

Student Leadership at Its Finest 学生领导力的最佳体现

Eric Zhang, Prime Minister of the Student Council, expressed his pride in the success of the event. "The talent show exemplified the incredible dedication and creativity of the students," Zhang said. "Our goal was to create a platform where everyone could express themselves and share their talents with the community. The outpouring of support from students and teachers was truly inspiring."

Zhang also highlighted the collaborative effort involved in organising the show. "Organising this event required a lot of teamwork and planning. From auditions to rehearsals, every member of the Student Council played a vital role. It was a challenging but rewarding experience."

学生会主席 Eric Zhang 对活动的成功表示自豪。“才艺秀展示了学生们的非凡奉献和创造力，” Eric 说。“我们的目标是搭建一个平台，让每个人都能表达自己，并与大家分享他们的才华。非常感谢学生和老师们的支持。”

Eric 还强调了组织该活动所需的协作能力。“组织这个活动需要大量的团队合作和详细的计划。从试镜到排练，学生会的每一个成员都发挥了重要作用。这是一次具有挑战性的任务。”

Director's Behind the Scenes 导演的幕后故事

A student is performing at the Talent Show.
一名正在进行表演的学生。

Sophie Yang, Director of the Talent Show, tells us about the careful planning and coordination required to make this event happen. "Directing the talent show is both exciting and demanding," says Ms Yang. "We want to make sure that every performance is perfect and that every performer feels confident and supported."

Yang emphasised the importance of inclusivity in the selection process. "We make a conscious effort to include a variety of performances. It is important that every student feels they have a place in our programme, regardless of their talent."

才艺秀导演 Sophie Yang 告诉我们，为了使该活动顺利进行，进行了精心的策划和协调。Sophie 说：“导演才艺秀既令人兴奋又富有挑战性。我们希望确保每一个表演都完美无缺，每一个表演者都能充满信心并得到必要的支持。”

Sophie 强调了选拔过程中的包容性重要性。“在筛选节目的阶段，我们有意识地涵盖各种表演。无论学生的才华如何，每个人都应该在我们的节日中找到自己的位置。”

Community and Creativity 集体与创造力

"Events like this bring us closer together. They remind us that CQSS is a vibrant and supportive community," he says. Director Yang echoes this sentiment, adding, "Seeing the joy and pride on the faces of the performers is very fulfilling. It is moments like these that make all the hard work worthwhile."

“这样的活动让我们更紧密地联系在一起，它们无不告诉我们CQSS是一个充满活力的集体。”他说。

Sophie 也表达了同样的感受，并补充道：“看到表演者脸上的喜悦和自豪非常令人欣慰。正是这样的时刻让所有的辛勤付出变得值得。”

Looking Forward 展望未来

As the curtains came down on the Summer Talent Show 2024, the applause and cheers from the audience clearly indicated the success of the show. The event not only provided a platform for students to showcase their talents, but also strengthened the bonds within the school community.

Eric Zhang and Sophie Yang both expressed their anticipation for future events. "We are already thinking about next year's show and how we can make it even better. Our goal is to continue to create opportunities for students to excel."

Ms Yang concluded, "The talent show is just a very basic example of what our students can achieve when given the opportunity to excel. I can't wait to see what the future holds for our talented group."

随着2024年夏季才艺秀的帷幕落下，学生和老师们的掌声和欢呼声清楚地表明了演出的成功。 这次活动不仅为学生提供了展示才华的平台，还加强了学校集体的凝聚力。

Eric Zhang 和 Sophie Yang 都表达了对未来活动的期待。 “我们已经在考虑明年的演出，如何让它变得更好。我们的目标是继续创造机会，让学生们有机会展示自己。”

Sophie 总结道：“才艺秀只是一个例子，我迫不及待地想看看我们才华横溢的同学们未来会有什么样的表现。”
