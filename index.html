课程设置

CQSS provides students with a high school programme for grades 10-12 in B.C. After completing a certain number of credits and graduating successfully, students can obtain a high school diploma and transcripts from both China and Canada. The B.C. high school programme is highly recognised globally, and students can apply to top universities in Canada, the U.S., the U.K., Australia and other countries directly after graduation with their three years of high school and IELTS scores.

青岛九中中加班为学生提供加拿大BC省10-12年级的高中课程，同学们在修满一定学分后顺利毕业，可获得中加两国的高中毕业证书与成绩单。 加拿大BC省高中课程含金量和全球认可度都非常高，学生顺利毕业后可直接凭借高中三年及雅思成绩申请加拿大、美国、英国、澳大利亚等国家的顶尖大学。

Our Curriculum
我们的课程体系

In terms of curriculum, in addition to giving students a complete education on the knowledge system of senior secondary school to meet the academic requirements of students applying for quality universities, CQSS pays more attention to the development of the seven core competencies of students, include Creative Thinking, Critical and Reflective Thinking, Communication and Collaboration, Personal and Social Awareness and Responsibility, Positive Personal and Cultural Indentity.
在课程设置方面，除了让学生接受完整的高中知识体系教育，以满足学生申请优质大学的学业要求外，中加学校更注重培养学生的七大核心能力，包括创新思维、批判与反思、沟通与协作、个人与社会意识与责任、积极的个人与文化个性。

Our host School

青岛市第九中学

Qingdao No.9 High School, Shandong, China

Qingdao No.9 High School, founded in 1900, is a century-old school of Shandong,
the first group of key secondary schools in Shandong Province, provincial standardised schools,
a high-standard boarding school with advanced concepts, excellent teachers, and good humanities and environment.
With 124 years of history, Qingdao No.9 High School has cultivated eight academicians.

青岛九中始建于1900年，是“名重齐鲁学界”的百年名校，
是山东省首批办好的重点中学、省级规范化学校，
是一所理念先进、师资优良、人文与环境俱佳的高标准寄宿制学校。
124年薪火相传、学脉赓续，
青岛九中先后培养出了八位院士，
素有“学在九中，院士摇篮”的美誉。
